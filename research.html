AI-Enabled Elderly Care

AI-Enabled Rural Revitalization

Selected publication:

Data-driven Governance

This project concerns the utilization and social impact of data-driven technologies in various domains of governance. Through long-term ethnographic study, we aim to understand what and how these data-driven technologies are implemented in practice and their real-world effectiveness and implications for governance, societal dynamics, and individual and social well-being.

Selected publications:

  • Yuchen Chen, Yuling Sun*, Silvia Lindtner. Maintainers of Stability: The Labor of China’s Data-Driven Governance and Dynamic Zero-COVID. Proceedings of the 2023 CHI Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems (CHI 2023, CCF-A).
  • Yuling Sun, Xiaojuan Ma, Silvia Lindtner, Liang He. Care Workers’ Wellbeing in Data-driven Healthcare Workplace: Identity, Agency, and Social Justice. Proceedings of the ACM on Human-Computer Interaction, 7(CSCW2) (CSCW 2023, CCF-A) (Recognition for Contribution to Diversity and Inclusion Award).
