Living in a fast-paced city often leads us to lose sight of lives and ourselves. Amidst the hustle and bustle of work, I like to find time to step out of the towering skyscrapers and see different worlds, meet different people, and experience diverse lives. Experiencing different parts of the world excites me. I have a passion for various sports, including running, mountain climbing, hiking, and cycling, and hope to experience the world through them.

I have a romantic lifestyle habit: whenever I arrive in a new city, I will run 10 kilometers along its streets in the early morning, and send myself a postcard from the local area. I hope to leave my footprints in different corners of the world and to capture the beauty of those places through postcards stamped with their unique marks. So far, I've collected over a hundred postcards! If you share this habit, let me know!! I'd be more than happy to send you one from a distant place! And if I could receive one from you too, I would be wild with joy :)

I also like to engage in various social volunteer activities, exchanging thoughts with different individuals, and immersing myself in varied experiences.
